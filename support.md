## Common questions

### Why does Sweeperbox need access to my Gmail?
We need read access to identify senders in your inbox, and modify access to apply the Trash / Important labels and create the unsubscribe filter when you tap Unsubscribe. We never read message bodies. Full details in the [Privacy Policy](./privacy).

### How do I cancel my subscription?
On your iPhone: **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Sweeperbox → Cancel Subscription**.
Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

### How do I get a refund?
Apple handles all refunds. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) and select your Sweeperbox purchase.

### How do I delete my account?
In the app: **Settings → Delete Account**. This permanently deletes your profile, linked Gmail accounts, sender history, and all action records. We also do a best-effort cleanup of Gmail filters we created.

### I unsubscribed from a sender by mistake. Can I undo?
Yes — go to the **History** tab, find the sender, and tap **Undo**. We delete the Gmail filter immediately. Any emails already trashed stay in your Gmail Trash, where you can restore them within 30 days.

### A sender I unsubscribed from is still emailing me. Why?
Some senders use multiple "From" addresses (e.g., `news@brand.com` and `marketing@brand.com`). Our filter blocks the exact address you swiped. If new variants appear, swipe them too.

### How does the free tier work?
You get 5 unsubscribes and 1 Gmail account on the free tier. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited unsubscribes and multiple accounts.

### How do I revoke Gmail access entirely?
Even after deleting your Sweeperbox account, you can revoke our app's Google access:
1. Go to [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Find "Sweeperbox"
3. Tap "Remove Access"

### Is my data safe?
All data is stored in our Supabase backend with encryption at rest and TLS in transit. We don't sell, share, or use your data for advertising. See the [Privacy Policy](./privacy) for full details.

## Bug reports

When emailing, please include:
- iOS version (Settings → General → About → iOS Version)
- App version (Sweeperbox → Settings → About → Version)
- A short description of what happened and what you expected
- Screenshots if relevant
