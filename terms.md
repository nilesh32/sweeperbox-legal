## Gmail actions

When you tap Unsubscribe, the app:
1. Creates a Gmail filter (via Google's official API) that auto-trashes future emails from that sender.
2. Optionally moves existing emails from that sender to your Trash.

You can undo any action from the History tab — the filter is removed and trashed emails remain in Trash (you can restore them in Gmail).

We are not responsible for emails you intended to keep that get filtered. Always verify the sender before unsubscribing. Use Undo if you change your mind.
